🍛 Chicken Tikka Curry

Creamy, spiced curry built on a yogurt marinade and slow-simmered sauce.

Quick Stats

⏱ 45–60 mins • 💪 High Protein • 🍽 3–4 servings

Ingredients

  • Chicken thighs
  • Greek yogurt
  • Garam masala
  • Ground cumin
  • Ground paprika
  • Garlic
  • Ginger
  • Onion
  • Canned tomatoes
  • Cream or coconut cream
  • Butter
  • Lemon juice

Method

  1. Marinate chicken:
    Mix chicken thighs with Greek yogurt, garlic, ginger, spices, and lemon juice. Let sit for at least 30 minutes (overnight is best).
  2. Brown chicken:
    Heat pan over medium-high heat and sear chicken until lightly browned. Remove and set aside.
  3. Build base:
    In the same pan, cook onion until soft and golden.
  4. Add aromatics:
    Add garlic, ginger, and spices. Cook for 1–2 minutes until fragrant.
  5. Tomato base:
    Add canned tomatoes and simmer. Optionally blend for a smoother sauce.
  6. Simmer chicken:
    Return chicken to pan and simmer until fully cooked and tender.
  7. Finish:
    Add cream, butter, and lemon juice. Adjust seasoning.

Nutrition

High protein, moderate fat, nutrient-dense and balanced meal.

Micronutrients

Protein (chicken), probiotics (yogurt), anti-inflammatory compounds (ginger), vitamin C (tomatoes, lemon), antioxidants (spices).

Tips

Don’t rush the marinade — it’s where most of the flavour comes from.
