🍚 Sticky Beef Mince Bowl

Sweet, savoury beef mince served over rice with a rich umami broth base.

Quick Stats

⏱ 25–30 mins • 💪 High Protein • 🍽 2–3 servings

Ingredients

  • Beef mince
  • Garlic
  • Onion
  • Soy sauce
  • Honey
  • Miso paste
  • Chicken stock
  • Rice

Method

  1. Brown beef mince until caramelised, then set aside.
  2. Sauté onion and garlic until soft.
  3. Add soy sauce and honey, letting it reduce slightly.
  4. Stir in miso and stock to create a light broth.
  5. Return beef and simmer until sticky and glossy.
  6. Serve over rice with broth spooned over.

Nutrition

High protein, moderate carbs, rich umami flavour.

Micronutrients

Beef (iron + zinc), miso (gut health), garlic (immune support), onion (antioxidants).
