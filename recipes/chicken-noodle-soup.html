🍜 Chicken Noodle Soup

Slow-simmered broth made from roast chicken with miso and aromatics.

Quick Stats

⏱ 2–3 hrs • 💪 High Protein • 🍽 4–6 servings

Ingredients

  • Whole roast chicken (carcass + meat)
  • Onion
  • Garlic
  • Ginger
  • Carrot (optional)
  • Miso paste
  • Noodles
  • Spring onion

Method

  1. Remove meat from roast chicken and set aside.
  2. Simmer carcass with onion, garlic, ginger and carrot for stock (1–2 hrs).
  3. Strain broth and return to pot.
  4. Stir in miso paste gently (don’t boil heavily).
  5. Add noodles and cook until tender.
  6. Add shredded chicken and warm through.
  7. Finish with spring onion.

Nutrition

High protein, gut-friendly, hydrating and restorative.

Micronutrients

Miso (gut health), chicken (protein), garlic (immune support), ginger (anti-inflammatory).
