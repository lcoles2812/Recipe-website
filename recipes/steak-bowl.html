🥩 Steak + Sweet Potato Bowl

Simple high-protein plate with chimichurri, roasted sweet potato and greens.

Quick Stats

⏱ 30–40 mins • 💪 High Protein • 🍽 1–2 servings

Ingredients

  • Steak (sirloin or ribeye)
  • Sweet potato
  • Broccolini
  • Garlic
  • Parsley
  • Olive oil
  • Vinegar or lemon juice

Method

  1. Sweet potato: Roast in oven until crispy outside and soft inside.
  2. Steak: Season and sear in hot pan. Rest for 5–10 minutes.
  3. Broccolini: Steam lightly, then sear in pan with garlic.
  4. Chimichurri: Mix parsley, garlic, olive oil, vinegar and salt.
  5. Assemble: Slice steak and serve with sweet potato, broccolini and chimichurri.

Nutrition

High protein, high micronutrient density, balanced carbs and fats.

Micronutrients

Iron (steak), potassium (sweet potato), vitamin C (broccolini), antioxidants (parsley).
