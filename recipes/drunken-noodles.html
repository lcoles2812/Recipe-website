🍜 Drunken Noodles

Bold Thai-style noodles with chilli, garlic, soy glaze and fresh basil.

Quick Stats

⏱ 25–30 mins • 💪 High Protein • 🍽 2–3 servings

Ingredients

  • Chicken thigh strips
  • Rice noodles
  • Garlic + ginger
  • Chilli / chilli flakes
  • Soy sauce
  • Oyster sauce
  • Fish sauce
  • Honey
  • Thai basil
  • Spring onion

Method

  1. Prep chicken: Slice and optionally marinate in soy, garlic and ginger.
  2. Cook noodles: Soak or boil until just tender. Drain and set aside.
  3. Cook chicken: Sear on high heat until browned. Remove.
  4. Aromatics: Fry garlic, ginger and chilli until fragrant.
  5. Build sauce: Add soy, oyster, fish sauce and honey. Let it bubble slightly.
  6. Combine: Add noodles and chicken back in. Toss until coated and glossy.
  7. Finish: Add Thai basil and spring onion off heat.

Nutrition

High protein, moderate carbs, flavour-dense and energising meal.

Micronutrients

Garlic (immune support), basil (antioxidants), chilli (metabolism support), chicken (protein + B vitamins).
