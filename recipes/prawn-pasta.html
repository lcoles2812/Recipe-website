🍤 Garlic Chilli Prawn Pasta

Light white wine pasta with garlic, chilli, lemon and fresh prawns.

Quick Stats

⏱ 20–25 mins • 💪 High Protein • 🍽 1–2 servings

Ingredients

  • Prawns
  • Garlic
  • Chilli flakes
  • White wine
  • Lemon juice
  • Pasta
  • Parsley

Method

  1. Cook pasta: Boil until al dente, save pasta water.
  2. Sear prawns: Cook quickly in hot pan until pink. Remove.
  3. Aromatics: Fry garlic and chilli gently.
  4. Deglaze: Add white wine and reduce slightly.
  5. Sauce: Add lemon juice and pasta water to create light sauce.
  6. Combine: Add pasta and prawns back in and toss.
  7. Finish: Parsley and seasoning.

Nutrition

Lean protein, low fat, high flavour, light digestion.

Micronutrients

Prawns (iodine + protein), garlic (immune support), lemon (vitamin C), parsley (antioxidants).
