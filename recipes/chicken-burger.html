🍔 Chicken Burger

Crispy crumbed chicken with fresh slaw and spicy creamy sauce.

Quick Stats

⏱ 25–35 mins • 💪 High Protein • 🍽 1–2 servings

Ingredients

  • Chicken breast
  • Bread crumbs
  • Egg
  • Red cabbage
  • Carrot
  • Onion
  • Greek yogurt
  • Mayo
  • Sriracha
  • Pickles (optional)

Method

  1. Crumb chicken using flour → egg → breadcrumbs.
  2. Fry or air-fry until golden and cooked through.
  3. Mix slaw ingredients with yogurt + mayo + vinegar.
  4. Mix sauce: mayo + yogurt + sriracha.
  5. Assemble burger with slaw, chicken, sauce and pickles.

Nutrition

High protein, moderate carbs, balanced fats.

Micronutrients

Cabbage (fibre + vitamin C), chicken (protein), yogurt (probiotics), carrots (beta carotene).
