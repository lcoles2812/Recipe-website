🍛 Panang Curry

Creamy, slightly sweet Thai curry with rich coconut and deep spice.

Quick Stats

⏱ 30–40 mins • 💪 High Protein • 🍽 3–4 servings

Ingredients

  • Chicken thighs
  • Panang curry paste
  • Coconut cream/milk
  • Garlic
  • Capsicum
  • Green beans
  • Fish sauce
  • Sugar (optional)

Method

  1. Slice chicken and sear in a hot pan until lightly browned.
  2. Add curry paste and fry it out for 1–2 minutes.
  3. Add coconut cream and stir until smooth.
  4. Add garlic, capsicum and green beans.
  5. Simmer gently until chicken is cooked through.
  6. Season with fish sauce and a small pinch of sugar if needed.

Nutrition

High protein, high fat, nutrient-dense and satisfying meal.

Micronutrients

Capsicum (vitamin C), coconut (healthy fats), chicken (protein + B vitamins), green beans (fibre).
