🌮 Pulled Beef Tacos

Slow-cooked chipotle beef packed with smoky depth and fresh toppings.

Quick Stats

⏱ 2–3 hrs • 💪 High Protein • 🍽 4–6 servings

Ingredients

  • Chuck beef
  • Chipotle in adobo
  • Onion
  • Garlic
  • Spices (cumin, paprika)
  • Beef stock
  • Taco shells
  • Pico de gallo

Method

  1. Blend chipotle, garlic, onion and spices into a paste.
  2. Sear beef until browned.
  3. Add paste and cook briefly to deepen flavour.
  4. Add stock and simmer low for 2–3 hours.
  5. Shred beef once tender and reduce sauce until thick.
  6. Serve in tacos with fresh pico.

Nutrition

High protein, rich flavour, slow-digesting energy.

Micronutrients

Beef (iron), tomatoes (vitamin C), spices (anti-inflammatory compounds), onion/garlic (immune support).
