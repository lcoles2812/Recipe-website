🌯 Burrito Bowl

Smoky chipotle chicken with fresh toppings, balanced rice bowl built for high protein and meal prep.

Quick Stats

⏱ 30–40 mins • 💪 High Protein • 🍽 3–4 servings

Ingredients

  • Chicken thighs
  • Chipotle in adobo sauce
  • Lime juice
  • Garlic + spices (cumin, paprika)
  • Rice
  • Black beans
  • Corn
  • Capsicum
  • Onion
  • Greek yogurt
  • Mayo (small amount)

Method

  1. Marinate chicken: Blend chipotle, garlic, lime juice and spices. Coat chicken and let sit (even 20 mins helps).
  2. Cook rice: Cook rice and season lightly with salt and lime juice.
  3. Cook chicken: Sear chicken in a hot pan until browned and cooked through. Rest then slice.
  4. Cook veg: Sauté capsicum and onion until slightly charred but still crunchy.
  5. Build sauce: Mix chipotle marinade with Greek yogurt and a little mayo + lime juice.
  6. Assemble: Rice base → chicken → beans → corn → veg → sauce.

Nutrition

High protein, fibre-rich, balanced carbs, gut-friendly fats.

Micronutrients

Capsicum (vitamin C), beans (fibre + magnesium), chicken (B vitamins), lime (antioxidants).
