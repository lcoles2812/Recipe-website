🍛 Chicken Tikka Curry

Creamy spiced curry with marinated chicken thighs, built for flavour and meal prep.

Quick Stats

⏱ 45 mins • 💪 High Protein • 🍽 3–4 servings

Ingredients

  • Chicken thighs
  • Greek yogurt
  • Garam masala
  • Garlic + ginger
  • Lemon juice
  • Onion
  • Canned tomatoes
  • Cream or coconut cream
  • Butter

Method

  1. Marinate chicken in yogurt, spices, garlic and lemon.
  2. Brown chicken in pan.
  3. Sauté onion, garlic, ginger and spices.
  4. Add tomatoes and blend (optional).
  5. Simmer chicken in sauce until tender.
  6. Finish with cream, butter and lemon juice.

Nutrition

High protein, moderate fat, low processed carbs.

Micronutrients

Garlic (immune support), ginger (anti-inflammatory), tomatoes (vitamin C, lycopene), yogurt (probiotics, calcium).

Tips

Let it sit overnight — flavour improves massively.
